# System 
1. search and get materials
   - here I use slides provided by school as an example
2. understand by exploring the slides one by one
   - the only goal while looking at each slide or a sequence of slides is to extract 1, or a few, very rough ideas the slides attempted to tell you
     - the major goal likely would always be broken down into small sub goals
     - For example,
       - solve that one single confusion you had while reading the slide
         - a single confusion might have layers and could be further broken down again
         - just keep breaking it down until it is digestible for you. For example, when a certain linkage could be formed with other similar concepts you learnt
         - simply speaking, identify the problem at a stage and find ways to solve it.
3. weave concrete understanding by verifying the rough ideas with 
   - problem drilling 
   - active recall
4. revision with:
   - more problem solving
   - active recall on white paper
