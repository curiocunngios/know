# Problem 
- A lamp has a switch and a bulb. The probability of failure for the switch is 0.05, and the
probability of failure for the bulb is 0.1. Assume the conditions of the switch and the bulb are
independent, what is the probability for the lamp to work properly?

-  If we do not assume the independence of the switch and bulb
(e.g., manufacturing quality, humidity), what is the probability for
the lamp to work properly in the worst case (i.e., its lower bound)?
