# Problem 
- There are two coins. When tossed, coin 1 shows tail with probability 1/10 and coin 2 shows tail with probability 1/2. We randomly pick a coin and tossed it twice.
  - What is the probability of getting a tail on the first toss?
  - What is the probability of getting tails on both tosses?
# Solution 
- 3/10
- 13/100
