# Problem 
- In a certain factory, three machines, I, II and III, make 30%, 45% and 25%,
respectively of the spring products. Of their production, machines I, II, and III, respectively produce 2%, 3%, and 2% defective springs. If one spring is selected at random from the total springs produced in a day. 

- Question: What is the probability that the selected spring is defective?

- Question: If the selected spring is defective, what is the conditional probability that it was produced by machine III?

# Solution
- 0.0245
- 10/49
