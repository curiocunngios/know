# LANG2030
- ## information
  - ## work
    - weekly:
      - 3 hours of class
      - 6 hours of work
        - expect more if you want to learn and improve more
    - final:
      - two group presentation
        - one on engineers ethics
        - one on engineering innovation
      - lead a seminar discussion
        - on engineering ethical issue
      - write analytical report (short)
      - individual proposal report
        - on engineering innovations
  - ## topics:
    - The communicating engineer:
      - learn engineers characteristics
        - do analysis and discussion 
      - analyze languages use in engineering texts
    - Engineers and social responsibility:
      - learn ethical issues for engineers
        - references to real-world cases 
    - Engineers and creativity:
      - work on engineering innovations
        - do: discuss and evaluate
        - work in group 
  - ## aim of the course:
    - train ability to:  
      - present and discuss
        - specifically for work relevant to a professional engineer
      - write
        - engineering-related topics
      - combine sources 
        - and weave own ideas
    - language-wise (ability to fulfill above goals):
      - develop related vocabularies (engineering, professional related)
      - structuring 
      - skills for
        - writing
        - speaking 
  - ILOs (intended-learning outcomes)
    - problem-solving
      - synthesis and substance:
        - able to:
          - critically discuss and analyze:
            - semi-technical topics
            - engineering issues
          - select relevant and appropriate information from semi-technical engineering texts
            - then summarize and synthesize
          - properly state sources
          - support claims with evidence
    - communication
      - coherence
        - able to:
          - fully and clearly develop well-framed ideas 
            - in both speaking and writing 
            - with relevant information, ideas and arguments
          - organize engineering-related ideas with 
            - good structures
            - good format 
      - language accuracy and use:
        - identify the needs and concerns of non-technical audience
        - able to adapt the way to communicate accordingly
        - have accurate (for engineering related tasks):
          - pronunciation
          - intonation
          - vocabulary 
          - linguistic structures, style
        - mode of communication:
          - able to use different modes;
            - audio
            - written
            - visual
          - have nice body language
          - interact effectively with people in:
            - seminar 
            - Q&A session 
      - personal development:
        - professionalism:
          - able to:
            - team work effectively
            - give good feedback to others (constructive)
      - social responsibility
        - cutural knowledge and ethical awareness 
          - evaluate engineering technologies (e.g. to society) 
            - benefits
            - danger
          - recognize ethical issues and career pressue of engineers

    - uses criterion-referencing (?)

      - the fact of the case
      - causes of problems
      - what harm has been done, to be caused
    - ### Determine
      - ethical issues from the case
      - how can it be defined
      - What is wrong 
      - What ethical code have b een breached
    - ### Discuss 
      - options for the stakeholders
    - ### Decide
      - evaluate each options
      - which options is the most ethical
